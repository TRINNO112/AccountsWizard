My Chaotic Journey with AccountsWizard

A timeline of my ups and downs, sleepless nights, Firebase fights, and one legendary teacher.

August 2025
In August, I started the development of AccountsWizard using React, Node.js, and Tailwind CSS. But kya kahun bro... meri kismat hi kharab thi. After writing thousands of lines of code, not even a basic text appeared on the screen. I was frustrated, demotivated, and honestly depressed. That month felt like a complete L. I wasn’t even in the mood to continue further.
September 2025
Phir ek chhoti si motivation ne mujhe zinda rakha. I took a hard step — deleted my entire project (yes, all of it 😭) and started again from scratch. This time, I went old-school with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It was risky but definitely my best decision ever. Slowly, things started working. Lessons took shape, bugs were still everywhere, but development was finally steady and satisfying.
Exams Time
Then came the storm — exams. I saw my results and... ouch. Few subjects went terribly wrong. I knew it was my fault — zyada focus coding pe tha, padhai pe kam. 😅 But then, something magical happened. Nirmit Sir, my Accounts teacher, motivated me like no one else. He said, “Marks aren’t everything. What matters is what you truly learn.” Those words hit hard. Thank you so much, Sir. If you’re somehow reading this, just know — your motivation saved this entire project. 🙏
October 2025
This month was pure chaos again. 😵 I started fixing major bugs, created multiple new pages, and revamped my old design completely. Switching to Firebase for database management felt like jumping into lava — tough, confusing, but worth it. Har din ek naya error aata tha, but har raat ek chhoti si victory milti thi.
Present
So here we are — the present. AccountsWizard abhi bhi complete nahi hai. It has 14 lessons out of 22, and haan, kuch MCQs out of syllabus bhi hain. Few features are still half-baked, kuch data needs rechecking, and some buttons might just be decorative. 😅 But maine apni taraf se puri koshish ki hai. If you find issues or bugs, please mail me — ya phir apni life ke kuch fun facts share kar dena. It’s always nice to know someone actually reached till the end of this page. 😂

Frequently Avoided Questions

Questions you didn’t ask, but I have answers anyway. 😏

Is this a proper accounting learning site?

+
Bro, it’s based on DK Goel syllabus — solid info. But it’s also chaotic, full of humor, and designed by a student. Think of it as learning with vibes, not a formal class. 🎯

Will this make me an accounting topper?

+
Probably not, bro. But you might become the person in class who actually understands things. Also, witness how one student used AI, jugaad, and sheer determination to build something from scratch.

Was building this easy?

+
Bro, I wanted to throw my laptop multiple times. Animations broke, Firebase gave trust issues, AI didn’t understand my intent. But eventually, things worked. The struggle was real, but the W feels amazing. 😎

Nice try. AI wouldn’t have made such organized chaos. Every bug, every weird animation, every last-minute patch is 100% human-made.
