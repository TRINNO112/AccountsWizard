My Chaotic Journey with AccountsWizard

A timeline of my villain arc, powered by bugs, deadlines, and one awesome teacher.

August 2025
My coding journey's villain origin story. I tried to act all pro with Node.js and component-based code without any real experience. Likha hazaron lines of code, but only a few things actually worked. It was giving... chaos.
September 2025
I ghosted the component-based life and went back to my ex, static HTML. It felt like a step back, but zaroori tha. Bugs were still throwing hands, and random errors made me question everything. But small wins kept the motivation alive.
Exams Time
Real talk: I bombed my exams, especially Economics. The L was massive and I was this close to deleting the whole project. But Nirmit Sir, the real MVP, provided the clutch support. He reminded me that learning is a marathon, not a sprint, and hyped me up to keep grinding on AccountsWizard. No cap, this project wouldn't exist without him.
October 2025
With a renewed main character energy, I started rebuilding. I was laser-focused on infographics and visuals to make things make sense. The bugs were still buggin', but seeing it all come together, piece by piece, was a W.
Present
So here we are. AccountsWizard is live, but it's forever a work in progress. This journey taught me patience, persistence, and that a good mentor is a life hack. This project is basically my whole learning curve, with a side of memes. üòÑ

Frequently Avoided Questions

Questions you didn't ask, but I have answers for anyway.

Is this a legit accounting course?

+
Define "legit." It's based on the DK Goel syllabus, so the info is solid. But it's more like a fun, chaotic study guide made by a fellow student, not a formal class. It's an experience, a vibe.

Will this website make me an accounting topper?

+
Probably not, being honest. But it might make you the person in your class who actually understands what's going on. And you'll see how one student used jugaad, AI, and Google to build something from scratch.

Bro, I wanted to throw my laptop out the window at least 20 times. Animations broke, Firebase gave me trust issues... AI never gets it right the first time. The struggle was real. But hey, we move.
