MY JOURNEY

Hey there! I'm Trinno Asphalt, a Grade 11 student who turned frustration into creation.

When I started learning accounting, I found textbooks boring and confusing. Walls of text, complicated terms, and zero engagement - it felt like grinding in Minecraft without any rewards!

So I thought: "What if learning accounting could be as fun as playing Minecraft?" That's when AccountsWizard was born. I combined my love for coding, gaming, and learning to build something that actually makes sense.

I wanted colorful visuals, interactive elements, and bite-sized lessons that don't put you to sleep. No more boring PDFs - just pure learning magic! ✨

This project taught me more than accounting - it taught me that if something doesn't exist, you can BUILD it yourself. Just like in Minecraft, you start with nothing and create something amazing.

AccountsWizard is my gift to fellow students struggling with accounting. If this helps even one person ace their exams while having fun, my mission is complete!
