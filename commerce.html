Your Learning Journey
Master Accounting in 6 Simple Steps

  • STEP 1
    Start Your Learning Adventure
    Your journey begins the moment you click Start Learning on the homepage or the Learn button in the navbar. You’ll be taken straight to the Lesson Hub — your personal accounting dashboard. From here, every topic, note, and quiz is just one click away. So take a deep breath, grab a notebook, and prepare to turn accounting concepts into clear, practical knowledge.
  • STEP 2
    Find Your Perfect Lesson
    Once inside the Lesson Hub, explore Lessons 1–14 (excluding 3 & 5). Each lesson represents a key milestone in the DK Goel curriculum. Use the integrated Search Bar to quickly locate lessons using topic names or keywords like “Journal Entry” or “Trial Balance.” You can even browse chapter summaries to understand which one to start next. The experience is designed for both beginners and quick revisers alike.
  • STEP 3
    Deep Dive into Interactive Notes
    Each lesson offers beautifully formatted notes, written in simple English and powered by animations, infographics, and interactive diagrams. You’ll find real-world examples that connect accounting theory to daily life — like how journal entries work in small businesses or startups. Scroll at your own pace, highlight key terms, and revisit sections anytime. This phase builds your foundation before moving to practical testing.
  • STEP 4
    Test Yourself with MCQs
    After mastering the lesson, jump into the 20-question MCQ set crafted for that specific topic. Every attempt you make is automatically saved, so you can track your learning history. If you perform better than before, your progress score updates instantly. Immediate feedback shows your correct and incorrect answers, helping you fix weak areas on the spot — it’s like having a mini teacher built right into your screen.
  • STEP 5
    Track Your Progress
    Your learning performance is never lost. Click the account icon in the navbar → select My Progress to open your Progress Dashboard. Here, your best scores, quiz attempts, and topic-wise performance graphs are displayed in a clear, visual layout. Whether you improve by 2% or 20%, every record is updated in real time. This lets you identify which chapters need a quick review before moving on.
  • STEP 6
    Level Up with Extra Practice
    Once you’ve mastered the basics, head to the Quiz Pages for more exciting practice. Play through themed modes like GuessTheAccount (retro game style), RapidFire (90s challenge mode), and ScenarioBased (cyberpunk simulation). You can also try True/False or the Ledger Game to sharpen your bookkeeping instincts. Each quiz type tests a different skill — speed, logic, and accuracy — making sure you don’t just learn accounting, but actually enjoy it.
